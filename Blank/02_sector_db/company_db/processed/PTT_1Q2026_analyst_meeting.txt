PTT in Action                               Summary of PTT Group’s actions to support Thailand
Stability and Transparency for the Nation   during the crisis and ensure national energy security
   1   Invested over THB 111 billion in the past five years to enhance reliability, flexibility of refineries, and invested in PTT trading
       capabilities and network globally

  2    Accelerated sourcing of alternative crude supplies from non–Middle East sources to ensure sufficient supply, despite
       higher prices, freight, insurance, and other related costs

  3    Crude shipments stranded in the Strait of Hormuz during the conflict may lead to excess supply once the Strait reopens, as
       alternative sources have already been secured, potentially requiring sales at below cost.

  4    Refinery maintained 100%+ utilization and maximized diesel output during the crisis (throughout March–April 2026) to meet
       domestic demand, despite higher operating costs and sub-optimal profitability

  5    Refineries and petrochemical plants operated at full capacity to support customers and ensure national security of supply

  6    Accelerated sourcing of new gas supplies and increased domestic gas, with maintenance of gas separation plants
       postpone to ensure uninterrupted supply with no gas bypass required
  7    Ensured liquidity readiness to manage oil and gas price volatility, while bearing over THB 230 billion in additional liquidity
       burden
           Margin calls for feedstock procurement amounted to approximately THB 63 billion

           Additional working capital for oil and gas procurement increased by approximately THB 137 billion

           Outstanding receivables from the Oil Fund related to price compensation totaled approximately over THB 35 billion

           As a result, financing costs increased by approximately THB 600 million per month or THB 7 billion per year

  8    Leadership in transparency across the entire supply chain, under principles of good corporate governance
