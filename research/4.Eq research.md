# ปัญหาการทำงานและโอกาสของ AI สำหรับ Equity Research Analyst

## Executive Summary
Equity Research Analyst ยังเสียเวลามากกับ "information processing" มากกว่าการสร้าง original insight: อ่าน filings, transcripts, press releases, consensus, comps, model drivers, แล้วแปลงเป็น update note หรือ initiation report ภายใต้ deadline สั้น โดยงานวิจัยปี 2025 ระบุว่า analyst reports หลังใช้ FactSet AI มีแหล่งข้อมูลเพิ่มขึ้น 40%, topical coverage เพิ่มขึ้น 34%, analytical methods เพิ่มขึ้น 25%, และ timeliness ดีขึ้น แต่ forecast errors เพิ่มขึ้น 59% ด้วย [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705]. จุดเจ็บหลักคือ earnings season, model update, transcript synthesis, data reconciliation, และการผลิต narrative ที่ต้องถูกต้องตาม compliance [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp]. AI agents มี value มากที่สุดใน "drafting + retrieval + checking" ไม่ใช่ "stock picking autonomy": งานวิจัย ERR 72 ฉบับพบว่า 78.7% ของคำถาม/statement archetypes ในรายงาน equity research สามารถ automate ได้ โดย 48.2% เป็น text-extractable และ 30.5% เป็น database-extractable [Pop & Spörer, arXiv, 2025-06-06, https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.18327]. ข้อจำกัดสำคัญคือ hallucination, data lineage, MNPI leakage, model auditability, disclosure, และความเสี่ยงที่ AI เพิ่มข้อมูลให้ analyst มากเกินกว่าจะ synthesize ได้ดี [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705]. ในไทย/SET-listed research หลักฐาน public ที่วัด workflow โดยตรงยังบางมาก; pain point จึงน่าจะคล้ายตลาด global แต่หนักกว่าใน small/mid-cap coverage เพราะข้อมูลภาษาไทย, analyst coverage ไม่สม่ำเสมอ, และ disclosure structure ไม่ standardized เท่า SEC filings [Stock Exchange of Thailand, Wikipedia/SET-sourced summary, 2025-06-12, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stock_Exchange_of_Thailand]. สรุปเชิง management consulting: deploy AI เป็น supervised research production layer พร้อม citation, source traceability, versioning, MNPI controls, และ analyst sign-off ไม่ใช่ให้ AI ออก recommendation เอง [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241].

## Day-to-Day Workflow and Time Allocation
Workflow ปกติของ equity research เริ่มก่อนตลาดเปิดด้วยการอ่าน market news, company news, macro data, sector news, และ earnings releases ของบริษัทที่ cover [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp]. ช่วง earnings season งานจะเปลี่ยนจาก relationship/news monitoring ไปเป็น quantitative-heavy process เช่น update actuals, revise assumptions, compare guidance, update valuation, และ publish note อย่างรวดเร็ว [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp]. แหล่งข้อมูลหลักของ workflow ได้แก่ Excel models, Bloomberg Terminal, FactSet, SEC/issuer filings, transcripts, broker consensus, และ internal research repositories [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp].

Initiation report ใช้ effort สูงกว่า update note เพราะต้อง build model from scratch, define investment thesis, create peer group, valuation framework, risks, industry overview, and disclosures; อย่างไรก็ดี ผมไม่พบ public benchmark ที่ reliable ในช่วง 2023-2026 ที่ระบุ "กี่ชั่วโมง/กี่วันต่อ initiation report" แบบ cross-firm [Data gap noted from source review; no public benchmark found in cited 2023-2026 sources]. Update note โดยเฉพาะ earnings update มี turnaround สั้นกว่า แต่มีความเสี่ยงเรื่อง speed-vs-accuracy เพราะ analyst ต้องตัดสินใจเร็วบนข้อมูลที่ยังเพิ่งออก [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].

Management call workflow มี 4 ขั้นตอนหลัก: preparation of questions, attendance or transcript review, extraction of tone/guidance/KPIs, และ translation into model or thesis change [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp]. Earnings call transcripts เหมาะกับ AI summarization เพราะเป็น long, unstructured financial text ที่มี facts and figures ซับซ้อน [Khatuya et al., arXiv/SIGIR, 2024-05-03, https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.06669].

## Key Pain Points Ranked By Frequency/Impact
1. **Data overload และ source fragmentation** – Analyst ต้องรวม filings, transcripts, news, pricing, consensus, channel checks, and proprietary notes จากหลายระบบ ซึ่งเพิ่ม information processing cost [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].
2. **Earnings season compression** – งาน model update, note drafting, sales talking points, and client calls มารวมกันในช่วงสั้น ทำให้ timeliness มีค่ามากแต่เพิ่มโอกาสผิดพลาด [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].
3. **Transcript and call synthesis** – Financial transcripts ยาว, unstructured, และมี nuance ด้าน guidance/tone/Q&A ที่ traditional summarization ยังทำได้ยาก [Khatuya et al., arXiv/SIGIR, 2024-05-03, https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.06669].
4. **Manual model maintenance** – Analysts ต้อง reconcile actuals, consensus, segment assumptions, share count, FX, commodity prices, and peer multiples ใน spreadsheets ซึ่งผิดพลาดง่ายและตรวจย้อนกลับยาก [Daily Tasks and Challenges of Equity Research Analysts, Investopedia, 2015-08-28, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082815/day-life-equity-research-analyst.asp].
5. **Compliance burden** – Research reports ต้องมี reliable information, reasonable basis, valuation explanation, risk presentation, conflict disclosures, and distribution controls [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241].
6. **Coverage pressure** – MiFID II unbundling reduced economics for external research, especially small-cap coverage, and UK reforms have debated reversing parts of the framework because of reduced coverage concerns [Reversing MiFID II research costs, Financial News London, 2024-12-04, https://www.fnlondon.com/articles/reversing-mifid-ii-research-costs-would-be-extremely-painful-says-jpmorgans-emea-funds-boss-1915f16c].
7. **Synthesis-cost paradox** – AI can increase information richness while making final judgment harder, as FactSet AI-adoption research found higher report richness but also 59% higher forecast errors [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].

## เครื่องมือปัจจุบันและช่องว่างที่ต้องแก้ไข
Bloomberg, FactSet, Refinitiv/LSEG, and Capital IQ cover core analyst workflow: data discovery, modeling, visualization, report production, market data, fundamentals, and M&A intelligence [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705]. Bloomberg is strong in real-time market data and broad terminal workflows, while FactSet has positioned Mercury as an AI workflow optimizer, Refinitiv focuses on Workspace data synthesis, and Capital IQ is strong in fundamentals and transaction intelligence [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].

The gap is not lack of data, but lack of integrated "decision workflow": analysts still need to know what to ask, verify lineage, reconcile conflicting sources, and translate outputs into a model and recommendation [The Bloomberg Terminal Is Getting an AI Makeover, WIRED, 2026-04-28, https://www.wired.com/story/the-bloomberg-terminal-is-getting-an-ai-makeover-like-it-or-not]. Bloomberg's ASKB beta was reported as available to about one-third of Bloomberg Terminal's 375,000 users, with Bloomberg emphasizing validation, citation checks, and conservative design because finance decisions cannot rely on faulty information [The Bloomberg Terminal Is Getting an AI Makeover, WIRED, 2026-04-28, https://www.wired.com/story/the-bloomberg-terminal-is-getting-an-ai-makeover-like-it-or-not].

AlphaSense, Tegus, and similar expert-call/transcript platforms help search and summarize large document sets, but vendor claims should be treated as productivity claims unless independently validated [AlphaSense valuation funding report, Wall Street Journal, 2026-06-00, https://www.wsj.com/business/entrepreneurship/market-research-firm-alphasense-clinches-7-5-billion-valuation-in-new-funding-round-bda34de4]. AlphaSense reportedly acquired Tegus for USD 930 million in 2024 and later acquired Carousel, an Excel-focused AI assistant, to automate spreadsheet and modeling workflows; this is vendor strategy evidence, not proof of realized analyst alpha [AlphaSense acquires Carousel, Business Insider, 2025-10-00, https://www.businessinsider.com/alphasense-acquires-carousel-ai-tool-for-excel-2025-10].

## ข้อจำกัดด้านกฎระเบียบ (Regulatory Constraints)
Reg FD prohibits selective disclosure of material nonpublic information to securities market professionals unless public disclosure occurs first or simultaneously, which makes management-call workflows sensitive when analysts receive non-public context [Regulation Fair Disclosure, SEC/Wikipedia summary of SEC rule, 2000-08-00, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulation_Fair_Disclosure]. FINRA Rule 2241 requires broker-dealers to maintain policies managing conflicts in research preparation, content, distribution, public appearances, and interactions with investment banking, sales and trading, subject companies, and customers [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241]. FINRA Rule 2241 also requires purported facts in research reports to be based on reliable information and requires recommendations, ratings, or price targets to have reasonable basis with valuation method and risk explanation [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241].

AI-assisted research must therefore preserve source lineage, evidence retrieval, analyst review, and final accountability because "AI said so" does not satisfy reliable-information or reasonable-basis standards [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241]. SEC charged Delphia and Global Predictions in March 2024 for false and misleading AI claims, with total civil penalties of USD 400,000, showing that firms must not overstate AI use or capability [SEC Charges Two Investment Advisers with Making False and Misleading Statements About Their Use of Artificial Intelligence, SEC, 2024-03-18, https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2024-36].

For MNPI handling, AI agents should be permissioned by data classification: public filings and transcripts can be broadly indexed, internal notes and management-call notes need entitlements, and any wall-crossed or confidential material must be excluded from general-purpose model context [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241]. Disclosure of AI use is not yet a single universal research-report requirement in the sources reviewed, but misleading statements about AI use can create enforcement risk under existing anti-fraud and marketing rules [SEC Charges Two Investment Advisers with Making False and Misleading Statements About Their Use of Artificial Intelligence, SEC, 2024-03-18, https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2024-36].

## พื้นที่ที่ AI สามารถสร้างคุณค่าได้
AI agents can add the most value in "research operations" rather than autonomous recommendations: filing parser, transcript analyst, model updater, comps monitor, citation checker, and compliance pre-review [Pop & Spörer, arXiv, 2025-06-06, https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.18327]. A filing parser can extract segment revenue, margins, capex, debt maturity, covenant language, related-party transactions, risk-factor changes, and MD&A deltas from 10-K/10-Q or annual reports with source-linked snippets [LEDGER benchmark, arXiv, 2026-06-11, https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13100]. A transcript agent can compare prepared remarks and Q&A against prior-quarter language, detect guidance changes, summarize management tone, and map comments to model drivers [Khatuya et al., arXiv/SIGIR, 2024-05-03, https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.06669].

A model-update agent can ingest actuals, reconcile line items to the analyst's existing model, flag broken formulas, update consensus deltas, and generate a variance bridge while keeping analyst sign-off [AlphaSense acquires Carousel, Business Insider, 2025-10-00, https://www.businessinsider.com/alphasense-acquires-carousel-ai-tool-for-excel-2025-10]. A comps agent can monitor peer multiples, estimate revisions, share-price moves, sector news, and corporate actions, then push only material changes to the analyst [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705]. A note-drafting agent can produce first-pass earnings notes with thesis, positives, negatives, valuation impact, and risks, but every statement should be source-cited and auditable [FINRA Rule 2241, FINRA, effective version 2019 onwards, https://www.finra.org/rules-guidance/rulebooks/finra-rules/2241].

For Thailand/SET-listed research, plausible AI value is higher in bilingual disclosure parsing, Thai-to-English management-comment synthesis, SET/mai filings extraction, and local news monitoring because global platforms may have weaker local-language normalization than U.S. SEC workflows [Stock Exchange of Thailand, Wikipedia/SET-sourced summary, 2025-06-12, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stock_Exchange_of_Thailand]. However, Thailand-specific adoption should start with public-data workflows and strict entitlements because management access and channel checks can create MNPI sensitivity similar to global markets [Regulation Fair Disclosure, SEC/Wikipedia summary of SEC rule, 2000-08-00, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regulation_Fair_Disclosure].

## ช่องว่างของข้อมูลและหลักฐานที่ขัดแย้ง
There is strong evidence that AI can automate a large share of research-report components, but weaker evidence that AI improves final forecast accuracy or investment recommendations [Pop & Spörer, arXiv, 2025-06-06, https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.18327]. The main conflict is that one study finds GPT-style financial statement analysis can outperform human analysts directionally on future earnings changes, while another study of FactSet AI adoption finds analyst forecast errors rose 59% after AI-assisted reports became richer and faster [Kim, Muhn & Nikolaev, arXiv, 2024-07-25, https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.17866] [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705]. This conflict should not be averaged because the tasks differ: one tests LLM prediction on standardized anonymous statements, while the other observes real analyst workflow and report production after domain-AI adoption [Kim, Muhn & Nikolaev, arXiv, 2024-07-25, https://arxiv.org/abs/2407.17866] [Xue, Zhang & Zhu, arXiv, 2025-12-12, https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.19705].

Public 2023-2026 data on time-to-produce initiation reports versus update notes is sparse, and most available time estimates are either older career sources or internal firm benchmarks not publicly disclosed [Equity Research vs. Investment Banking, Investopedia, 2015-08-26, https://www.investopedia.com/articles/personal-finance/082615/careers-equity-research-vs-investment-banking.asp]. Vendor claims from AlphaSense, Bloomberg, FactSet, and similar platforms often describe workflow acceleration, but independent performance evidence is uneven and should be separated from marketing claims [SEC Charges Two Investment Advisers with Making False and Misleading Statements About Their Use of Artificial Intelligence, SEC, 2024-03-18, https://www.sec.gov/newsroom/press-releases/2024-36]. Thailand/SET-specific empirical workflow research is especially limited in public English sources, so recommendations for Thailand should be validated through interviews with local brokers, asset managers, SET/mai IR teams, and compliance officers [Stock Exchange of Thailand, Wikipedia/SET-sourced summary, 2025-06-12, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stock_Exchange_of_Thailand].
